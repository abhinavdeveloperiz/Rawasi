About Rawasi Al Ammal

Project Management & Electrical Services

Our History

Rawasi Al Ammal Contracting Company was established in the year 2010 and is based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

Within a short span of time, the company earned a strong reputation for excellence, quality, and quick response.

Rawasi Al Ammal Contracting is a standard-grade Project Management and Electrical Services firm with a valid license, organized with advanced technologies and highly skilled workers using sophisticated tools.

Our Philosophy

Over the years, the group has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills, know-how and experience in design and build solutions, project management services, building trades and related engineering works.

Project Management & Execution Philosophy

  • Create detailed schedules and resource plans to meet client projects
  • Communicate clearly with all project stakeholders
  • Track project progress and fine-tune deviations
  • Ensure only experienced and qualified professionals serve clients

Our Mission

To procure projects at competitive pricing, provide safe working conditions and deliver quality work within a reasonable time frame.

Our Values

  • Teamwork
  • Quality Service
  • Time-bound Delivery
  • Customer Satisfaction

Our Vision

To be a respectable building contractor delivering beyond expectations, always.

Our Team

The management team is involved in business development, procurement, project management and administration of the company. They continuously review processes and communicate key learning points and company policies to all staff.

Quality Policy

The company firmly believes in Quality Assurance and Quality Control. These concepts ensure that products and services meet stated and implied needs.

Quality Assurance involves systematic actions to provide confidence in quality, while Quality Control includes operational techniques to fulfill quality requirements, following safety rules and regulations.
